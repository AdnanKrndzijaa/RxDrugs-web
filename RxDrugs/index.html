Why RxDrugs?

RxDrugs has a very rich archive of medicines available in Bosnia and Herzegovina, as well as a system for keeping private inventory for drugs in your home pharmacy.

About us

The RxDrugs platform is designed to help peple learn something new about chemical and herbal supstances. RxDrugs was first of all made to make it easier for the users to access information by having everything in one place.

Simplicity

The simplicity of the RxDrugs app is in the way of searching the essetials about available drugs in Bosnia and Herzegovina.

Accessibility

The accessibility of the app is the biggest advantage, because it is free for all users and all data are publicly available for everyone.

Quality

All data about drugs are correct and taken from the official register of Bosnia and Herzegovina.

Drugs

The archive of available drugs in Bosnia and Herzegovina is subject to contant changes due to constant changes in the laws specifying the use of these substances. You can check this huge drug database in our mobile app, with all details like how to use a drug, what is the daily dose, max single dose, manufacturer, inner active supstances, ...
