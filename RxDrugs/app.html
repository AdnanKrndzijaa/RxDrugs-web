Services

The RxDrugs mobile app is designed to help the user research medications, all with local data storage, which means it's fully accessible even without an internet connection. RxDrugs is everything you've needed for years, as a digital database of available medicines in Bosnia and Herzegovina, but also wider, with the fact that it also has a local database of drugs that the user manually enters into the system. That inventory of drugs is not available, but soon users will have that additional option, with will certainly make their lives easier.

The application also has an option to quickly dial the numbers of emergency services in Bosnia and Herzegovina, also fully accessible without an internet connection, which additionally makes the application more user-friendly. In addition to these great possibilities that users have, there is also RxDrugs Zombie, or RxZombie for short, a program that provides users with rarely available information about drugs that were previously available, but are not available today, as well as substances that have been created by scientists recently. RxZombie has a special importance as an application, because it is not currently available, but it will definitely be available in one of the next versions and thus capture the attention of our users. In addition to very interesting content, it also enables access to highly protected data and documents that are difficult to access on the Internet.

All Drugs

Within the All Drugs section, users have the opportunity to read more about the types of drugs (CNS Depressants, CNS Stimulants, Opioids, Hallucinogens and Inhalants). In addition to definitions and basic information, users will be able to read examples of these drugs, as well as a link to the website, where everything is described in more details. In addition to the types of medicines, there is also a database of all available medicines in Bosnia and Herzegovina. Drugs are divided into two groups: OTC Drugs (drugs that can be bought and taken without a doctor's prescription) and Prescription Drugs (drugs that cannot be taken without a doctor's prescription; with a doctor's prescription, depending on the drug, some are obtained for free if are on the list reimbursed by the Institute for Health Insurance of Bosnia and Herzegovina, those that are partially reimbursed and those that must be paid at full price)

Inventory

Drug Inventory is a Premium option of the RxDrugs mobile app, as it allows users to keep track of their medications in their home pharmacy. This additional option is only available to a limited number of users and must be requested. Your drugs can be digitally stored and always available for viewing the current state and quantity, it is very easy to search and has the option of editing the added drugs, automatically reducing the amount of drug with one touch, as well as increasing. The drug database is local, so you don't need an internet connection in this case either. However, there will be soon a prototype of the functionality with the option of creating an account, in order to send the data to the main database in order to preserve the data in case of data loss locally on your device.

RxDrugs Zombie

RxDrugs Zombie or RxZombie is part of the mobile version of the Android application, which is designed for long-term learning and education for users who like to research and educate themselves in this direction. What RxZombie offers is a lot of content that includes various texts, hard-to-find information on the Internet and very interesting facts. In this section, you will find all the information about drugs that were used before, but are now either banned or practically no longer used because there are much better substances. Also, what will adorn RxZombie, is the section on narcotics, as well as the eductive articles, a lot of material that goes to the very core of each substance that is on the list. RxZombie is currently in the process of being redesigned, so it is currently unavailable to users. With the new version of the application, it will be open and available again, so I hope that it will encourage certain users to research something they may not have known about before.
